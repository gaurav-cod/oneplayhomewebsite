Here you can find more about the terms

In-order to test your own eligibility before applying, please ensure that you have a minimum of 20 total posts regardless of your platform, with 2 uploads/live-streams per week on YouTube or Twitch and at least 4 weekly posts on Instagram. Meeting this criteria gives you a chance of receiving free access to our platform to aid in your videos and grow your page/channel. Please note that in order to receive a creator discount code and early access to new features, you must meet a minimum followers/subscribers and viewer counts.

There is no harm in applying for the partnership even if you do not meet the eligibility criteria right now. We hope to provide an opportunity for our partners to grow along with our platform, and our review process looks at a number of internal measures to determine what level every partnership should function at.

Our partners are free to choose the kind of content they wish to create using our platform. While it is prefered that you have gaming and/or tech realted content uploaded on your page/channel before applying, we are happy to facilitate creators wishing to transition into the field. Please share your ideas in the application form below.

Upon partnering with us, a creator will be expected to upload content about our platform regularly, and add links to every video/post on a dashboard unique to the creator. The benefits every creator receives will grow with their page/channel size as long as they keep updating their dashboard.

All partnership applications submitted are subject to a review by our moderation team in order to determine whether they qualify for partnering with us at various levels.

All social media accounts in the form must be linked to the email account you provide and must be verifiable by a third party.

All applicants must have an existing OnePlay account but are not required to have an active subscription plan.
